Data deletion

Vacation Planner · How to delete your data

This page explains how to delete personal data associated with Vacation Planner, including data created after signing in with Facebook or another identity provider.

What we delete

  • Your Cognito user account (email and social-linked identity records for this app).
  • Trip and itinerary records stored for your user id in our DynamoDB table.

How to request deletion

  1. Email the project maintainer from the address you used with Vacation Planner (or the email on your Facebook account if that is how you signed in). Use the contact method listed in the public GitHub repository for this project.
  2. Use the subject line Vacation Planner data deletion request and include:
    • The email address on your Vacation Planner / Cognito account
    • Approximate dates you used the app (if known)
    • Confirmation that you want account and trip data permanently deleted
  3. We will delete your Cognito user and associated trip records, then reply when deletion is complete. Typical turnaround is within 7 days.

Facebook Login

Removing Vacation Planner from your Facebook Apps and Websites settings stops future Facebook login to this app. It does not by itself delete trip data already stored in our systems — follow the request steps above for that.
