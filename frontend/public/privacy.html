Privacy Policy

Vacation Planner · Last updated: July 22, 2026

Vacation Planner helps you plan trips. This policy describes what we collect, why we collect it, and how you can control it. This is a development / demo product.

Information we collect

  • Account data — If you sign in (email/password or a social provider such as Facebook or Google via Amazon Cognito), we receive identifiers and profile fields the identity provider shares (for example name, email, and a stable user id).
  • Trip data you create — Trip details, cities, day plans, preferences, and related content you enter in the app, stored in our backend (Amazon DynamoDB).
  • Technical logs — Limited operational logs from hosting and AI planning services (for example request metadata and error diagnostics). We do not sell this data.

How we use information

  • Authenticate you and keep your trips associated with your account.
  • Generate and improve itinerary suggestions using AI services you invoke in the app.
  • Operate, secure, and debug the service.

Sharing

We use cloud processors (notably Amazon Web Services: Cognito, API, DynamoDB, and Bedrock / AgentCore) to run the product. Social login providers (for example Meta / Facebook) receive only what is needed for sign-in when you choose that option. We do not sell personal information.

Retention

Account and trip data are kept while your account exists or until you request deletion. Logs may be retained for a limited operational period.

Your choices

You can request deletion of your account and trip data by following the instructions on our Data deletion page.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the project maintainer using the contact method listed in the public GitHub repository for this Vacation Planner project.
